PART 5 = RKSD College
: Admission & Eligibility
Admission Process (Short Steps)
1.	Online Registration: Candidates must register and fill out the application form on the official centralized admission portal of the Directorate of Higher Education, Haryana, for undergraduate (UG) courses. For postgraduate (PG) courses, the process is handled either directly through the college or the Kurukshetra University portal.
2.	Merit List: The college prepares and releases a merit list based on the marks obtained in the qualifying examination (Class 12 for UG, Bachelor's degree for PG). This list is displayed on the college notice board and website.
3.	Counselling and Document Verification: Shortlisted candidates are called for online or physical counselling, depending on the academic session's guidelines. During this stage, documents are verified.
4.	Fee Payment and Confirmation: After successful document verification, selected candidates must pay the admission fee within the specified time to confirm their seat. 
Eligibility Criteria (General)
•	For UG Courses (e.g., B.A., B.Sc., B.Com, BBA, BCA): Must have passed the 10+2 examination from a recognized board. Minimum marks vary depending on the course and category, but generally, it is around 45%–50%.
•	For PG Courses (e.g., M.A., M.Sc., M.Com): Must hold a relevant Bachelor's degree from a recognized university. Minimum marks generally fall between 45%–50%. 
Documents Required
•	Class 10th Marksheet and Certificate.
•	Class 12th Marksheet and Certificate.
•	Graduation Marksheet and Degree (for PG admissions).
•	Character Certificate from the institution last attended.
•	Transfer Certificate (if applicable).
•	Caste Certificate (for reserved categories).
•	Recent passport-size photographs.
•	Aadhaar card.
•	Anti-Ragging Undertaking (to be submitted online).
Fee Structure Overview
The fee structure varies by course, but generally falls within the following ranges for the 2025-26 session, as per reports: 
•	UG Courses: Approximately ₹15,000 to ₹20,000 annually.
•	PG Courses: Approximately ₹20,000 to ₹25,000 annually.
•	Specific fees for self-financed courses like BCA and BBA may be slightly higher. 
Admission Dates / Timelines (Approximate for 2025-26)
•	UG Online Registration: May–June 2025.
•	UG Merit List: June–July 2025.
•	UG Counselling/Admission: July–August 2025.
•	PG Online Registration: June–July 2025.
•	PG Merit List: July–August 2025.
•	PG Counselling/Admission: August 2025. 
Application Portal Link
•	For UG courses: Apply via the centralized portal of the Directorate of Higher Education, Haryana: https://admissions.highereduhry.ac.in.
•	For PG courses: The application link will be available on the RKSD College website: https://rksdcollege.ac.in.
