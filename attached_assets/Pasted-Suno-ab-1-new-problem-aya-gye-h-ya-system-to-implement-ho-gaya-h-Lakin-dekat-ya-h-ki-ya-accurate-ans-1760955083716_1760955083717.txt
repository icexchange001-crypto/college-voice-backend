(PG) College, Kaithal offers a total of 28 courses at the undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG), diploma, and certificate levels. The courses are available in a variety of disciplines, including Arts, Commerce, Science, and Information Technology. Undergraduate (UG) Courses Bachelor of Arts (B.A.): A three-year full-time course available in both morning and evening sessions. It includes subjects such as English, Hindi, Political Science, Economics, and History. Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.): A three-year program available in both morning and evening sessions. The specializations offered include: B.Com. (Honours) B.Com. (Taxation) B.Com. (Insurance) Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.): A three-year course offered in the morning session. Specializations include: Medical Non-Medical Computer Science Electronics Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA): A three-year, full-time course offered in the morning session. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA): A three-year, full-time course available in the morning session. Postgraduate (PG) Courses Master of Arts (M.A.): A two-year course with specializations in: English (Morning and Evening sessions) Hindi (Morning and Evening sessions) Political Science (Morning and Evening sessions) Economics (Evening session) Master of Commerce (M.Com.): A two-year course available in the evening session. Master of Science (M.Sc.): A two-year course offered in the morning session. Specialization: Mathematics Diploma and Certificate Courses Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications (PGDCA): A one-year course available in the evening session. Post Graduate Diploma in Yoga (PGD in Yoga): A one-year course offered in the morning session.
